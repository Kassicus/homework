Help & Documentation

Documentation and guidance for using the internal contract management platform.

Quick Start Guide

Use your internal credentials to access the platform. Contact your system administrator if you need access.

Note: Access is restricted to authorized internal users only.

Start by adding external companies to the system. Go to the Clients section and click "New Client" to enter their information including name, organization, contact details, and address.

Tip: The more complete your client information, the better you can track and manage your relationships.

Now you're ready to create contracts! Navigate to the Contracts section and click "New Contract". Fill in the contract details, select the client, and upload any supporting documents.

Important: Set expiration dates and reminders to never miss a renewal deadline.
